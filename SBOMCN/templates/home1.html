Symptom-Based Optimized Medical-Centre Navigator
This project aims to create a machine learning-based system that helps patients with rare diseases and uncommon treatments find appropriate healthcare options. The system will provide a user-friendly interface where patients can enter their symptoms or treatment name and receive a list of hospitals meeting their search criteria in terms of cost and location optimization. The risks include data availability, algorithm accuracy, and user adoption. The proposed system has the potential to revolutionize healthcare delivery and improve health outcomes for patients with rare diseases and uncommon treatments.
Guide to Use the Website

1. Enter your symptoms or desired treatment in the search bar.

2. Use filters to refine your search based on location, budget, and hospital rating.

3. Explore the list of recommended hospitals and their treatments.

4. Select your preferred hospital and treatment, and book an appointment directly on the website.
